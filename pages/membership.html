Membership

The Manteca Museum is operated by volunteer docents, board members and committee volunteers. The Museum receives no government funding and is a nonprofit organization.

Membership is the single most important source of funding for the museum each year, representing nearly half of the annual operational revenue budget, in addition to Memorial contributions, gift shop sales, and other fundraising initiatives.

Members gain full-calendar-year access to the Museum for family and friends. Your annual membership also helps keep the Museum open for free to the general public, and school children, all benefitting from your support.

Members receive a monthly newsletter, produced and mailed to their homes, or it can be acquired online at www.TheMantecaMuseum.org, or www.Manteca-Museum.org in the Newsletter section, if preferred. We also have some Members who receive an email sent to them, if email delivery is requested.

Members receive a 10% discount on all merchandise purchased in the Museum Gift Shop. Your name and membership will also be recognized in an upcoming newsletter.
