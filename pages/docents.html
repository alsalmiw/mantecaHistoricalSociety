A Call for Docents!

Founded in 1990 as a local non-profit, the Museum contains a vast amount of local history offered to it’s Membership and the General Public as one of Manteca’s entertainment and learning attractions! We have an urgent need for more volunteer docents for 2019 to help share Manteca’s Museum with visitors by scheduling a few hours a month to open the museum doors, welcome guests and tend to the museum gift shop! You can also help out by keeping things tidy and even volunteering for other committees if you have more time to spare! No prior experience is necessary. You learn as you go as we pair you up with other museum docents.

Museum hours are: Sundays 1pm to 4pm. We have openings for several shifts for 2022, especially Sundays. We hope to hear from you soon about joining us as a volunteer! Contact us by calling: Tommie Benavides at 209.275.7392, Vicki Miller at 209.823.4327, or just stop in during museum hours and talk with some of the docents on duty. You can enjoy the people, the history, and the spirit of helping to keep our museum alive and well for area visitors and historical society members!

Requirements

Requirements: You must be 18 or over, be friendly to people, be able to volunteer for at least one 2-3 hour shift monthly (12 month commitments are preferred), and you must be a yearly member of the Manteca Historical Society at any level! We appreciate all members, so member levels are discretionary, but it shows you care! Our current member/donor-levels recognized are: Students $5, Singles $15, Couples $25, Family $50, Sustaining Members $100, Organizations $100, Business Friend $250, Major Sponsor $500, Benefactors $1000 and Life Member $5000. All new and renewing memberships are published in our monthly newsletter both mailed and posted on our website.

Come join us! We welcome all newcomers to learn about Manteca, too!

If you have an interest in becoming a Docent or Volunteering for other Museum events, please contact the museum at 209.825.3021, or call Tommie Benavides, docent chair, at 209.275.7392.
